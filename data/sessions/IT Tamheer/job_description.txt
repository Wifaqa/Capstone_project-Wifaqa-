Overview:
The IT Tamheer is designed for recent graduates who are eager to kickstart their careers in the IT industry & eligible for Tamheer program. Trainees will gain practical experience and exposure t various IT functions, enhancing their skills and knowledge in professional environment.
Key Responsibilities:
*   Assist in IT support tasks, including troubleshooting hardware and software issues.
*   Collaborate with the IT team on system maintenance and updates.
*   Support the implementation of new technologies and IT projects.
*   Monitor and maintain network systems, ensuring reliability and performance.
*   Participate in the testing and evaluation of new software applications.
*   Document IT processes and procedures for knowledge sharing.
*   Provide training and support to end-users on IT systems and applications.
*   Engage in team meetings and contribute to ongoing projects.
Qualifications:
*   Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.
*   Strong interest in IT and eagerness to learn new technologies.
*   Basic understanding of computer networks, operating systems, and software applications.
*   Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.
*   Strong communication skills, both verbal and written.
*   Ability to work collaboratively in a team environment.
What We Offer:
